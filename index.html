Uzi

The inventor of the machine gun UZI1 was born as Gotthard Glas in the Weimar, Germany in 1923. After moving to Kibbutz Yagur in the British Mandate of Palestine he changed his name to Uziel Gal2. In 1951 the newly created Israeli Army started to use his invention and called it the UZI. Gal requested that it be called something else but the request was denied.

  1. Hebrew: עוזי‎

  2. Hebrew: עוזיאל גל
